Kya Sochta Hai Ae Dil
Sadak


kya sochata hai ai dil kya savaal karata hai
ye pyaar to sabhi ka bura haal karata hai
kya sochata hai ai.jisane pyaar kiya dil ka etabaar kiya
usane to khud hi apana bas jeena dushvaar kiya
pahate to dil laga ke phir dard meetha pa ke kya kamaal karata hai
ye pyaar to sabhi ka.jaata hai ik nazar mein dil ka sukuun saara
deta jo bekaraari lagata hai vo hi pyaara
kya ham bataayen kaise mat poochho kaise kaise kya kamaal karata hai
ye pyaar to sabhi ka.
