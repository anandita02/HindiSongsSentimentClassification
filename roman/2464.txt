Aati Hai To Chal
Saat Rang Ke Sapne


aati hai to chal mere saath mein
de de saathiya haathon ko haath mein
rehna hai har pal ab tere saath mein
le le saathiya haathon ko haath mein
mor ke sang hai morni saawan ke saath ghata
nadi ke sang naanv hai mausam ke saath hawa
aati hai to chaltu jo muskuraye khil jaayen gunche gulzaar ke
teri chotiyon mein raani mehkein phool bahaar ke
par hain suhane tere meethe meethe bol re
aa re ja o bhanvare na aage peechhe dol re
zor pe hai purvaiya dupatta ud na jaaye
kalaai tu jo pakde meri chuudi sharmaaye ho ho
aati hai to chalmere sapanon ki paayal chhanke gore paanv mein
zara der baithein aaj ham palakon ki chhaanv mein
saanson mein sitaar pe hamjoli tera naam hai
teri meri preet ki paawan sindoori shaam hai
door waadi mein jaaye kisi dulhan ki doli
saje hain armaan aaise saji jaisi rangeeli
aati hai to chal
