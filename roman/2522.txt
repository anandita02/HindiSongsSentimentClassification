Saat Suron Ke Taar Ban Gaye
Sangeet


saat suronn ke taar ban gaye teraa meraa pyaar
main huun naiyaa tuu patavaar paar kar lenge ham majhadhaar
saat suron ke taar ban gayeshaam hai apani apanaa saveraa
aur karenge kyaa karake ab kar liyaa tere dil mein baseraa
aur dishaa dishaa mein guunj uthi paayal ki jhanakaar
main huun naiyaameraa pag naache to naache naach uthaa vo sab alabelaa
kitanaa achchhaa lagataa hai na tuu akeli na main akelaa
mere badan ko chhed rahe hain laakhon sitaar
main huun naiyaa
