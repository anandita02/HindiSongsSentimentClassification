Ek Yaar Manga Tha
Officer


( Ek Yaar Manga Tha )ik yaar maanga tha maine rab tujhase
to kaun si khudaai maang li
ik katara chaaha tha maine saagar se tere
to kaun si khudaai maang li vo teri bekaraari mera deevaanapan
badhati hi ja rahi thi vo pyaar ki agan
haathon mein tera chehara palaken jhukeen jhukeen
honthon par dil ki baaten a kar rukeen rookeen
ik phool maanga tha bahaaron se teri
to kaun si khudaai ... chaahat ke raaston mein dil tanha rah gaya hai
khvaabon ka vo samandar aankhon mein bah gaya hai
use dhoondhati hain aankhen har gaam har dagar
har aahat dekhati hai ab tak meri nazar hamasafar
ik jhonka maanga tha havaao se teri
to kaun si khudaai ...
