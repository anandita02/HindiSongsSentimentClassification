Tumse Hai Kita Pyar Ye Hum Keh Nahin Sakte
Jeena Marna Tere Sang


tumase hai jitana pyaar ye ham kah nahin sakate
tumase bichhad ke ham to zinda rah nahin sakate
o mitava tujhe mera pyaar pukaare a re a re a re
tumase hai jitana pyaaro mitava tujhe mere git pukaarein a re a re a remere saanson ke aangan mein teri hai tasvir sanam
main teri taqadir aur tu meri hai taqadir sajan
teri khaatir to duniya ki rasmon ko ham todenge
chaahe chhuute saari duniya tujhako na chhodenge
tum mujhako milate ho jaise apanaapan sa lagata hai
tere pyaar ki baahon mein mujhako jivan sa lagata hai
teri aankhon mein din mera zulfon mein hai shaan lage
jivan jisako kahate hain vo tera naam lage
o mitava tujheo mitava tujhe
