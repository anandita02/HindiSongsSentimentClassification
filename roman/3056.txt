Yeh Kya Ho Raha Hai


hamane ki aarazu to hamein kya mila
o pyaar ki manzilen paayega dil tera
jhoothe saare khvaab hamein na dikha
sachche tere khvaab hain pal do pal thahar ja
ho hamane ki aarazu.nadiyaan khud chal ke pyaason ke paas aayengi
ban ke kaali ghata zulfen laharaayengi
nadiyaan aate aate rasta badal jaati hain
badaliyaan barase bin aage nikal jaati hain
ho paayegi zindagi ek din har khushi
jhoothe saare khvaab.sapanon ki nagari mein dil to gaya tha bahal
ab ye jaana ki the ret ke vo mahal
dil ke raste pe karate raho tum safar
agale hi mod pe pyaar ka hai nagar
o aasamaan ye zameen kuch nahi kuch nahi
sachche tere khvaab.
