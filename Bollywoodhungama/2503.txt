tak dhin dhin tak
milate hi tujhe.aisaa mausam to aaya na tha aisa jaadu to chhaaya na tha
aisi haalat to pahale na thi
kushiyaan jaag utheen man mein ab na gam hai jeevan mein
ji chaahe main ud jaaoon ambar chhu ke main aaoon
o o jeene ki tamanna.duniya badali si lagane lagi soi dhadakan kyon jagane lagi
rang mausam badalane laga mera dil bhi machalane laga
sachcha ho gaya sapana mujhako mil gaya apana
aramaanon ki basti mein ab rahana hai masti mein
o jeene ki tamanna.
