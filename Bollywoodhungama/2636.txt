Bahut Khoobsurat Ghazal
Shikari


bahot khubasoorat gajal likh rahaa hoo
tumhe dekhakar aajakal likh rahaa hoomile kab kahaa,kitane lamahe gujaare
mai gin gin ke wo saare pal likh rahaa hootumhaare jawaan khubasoorat badan ko
taraashaa huaa yek mahal likh rahaa hoon poochho meree bekaraaree kaa aalam
mai raaton ko karawat badal likh rahaa hoo
