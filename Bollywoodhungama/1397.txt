Kal Maine Khuli Aankh Se Ek Sapna Dekha
Jeena Marna Tere Sang


kal maine khuli aankh se ik sapana dekha
bhola pyaara chaand sa ek mukhada dekha
hai vo laakhon mein ek hasina dil mera jisane hai chhina
kahate hain sab usako pyaar se aasha
aasha i love you.maine bhi ik pyaara pyaara sapana dekha
par sapane mein alabela ek ladaka dekha
jaadu kiya hai usane dil chhin liya hai usane
kahate hain sab usako pyaar se jaanam
jaanam  i love you.is dil ki tum dhadakan ho dhadakan mein chhupa pyaar ho
ik aramaan ho mere dil ki aas ho meri saanson ki jhankaar ho
is dil ki tum chaahat ho chaahat mein chhupa raaz ho
saragam ho tum meri ik nagma ho tum mere dil ki aavaaz ho
jaanam  i love you.pyaara samaan ye dil hai javaan aaj chup hain zamin aasamaan
nazarein jo milin ye kaliyaan khilin ban gayi ik nayi daastaan
ho ye mastiyaan yaara ye shokhiyaan mujhe lagane lagi hain
ab tum jo mile mujhe aisa laga jaise bahaar a gayi
aasha i love you.
