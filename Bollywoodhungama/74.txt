Kitne Dinon Ke Baad Hai Aayee
Aayee Milan Ki Raat


kitne dinon ke baad hai aayi sajna raat milan ki
ab hamse na sahi jaaye judaai aayi raat milan ki
kitne dinon ke baad ...chanda pyaasa taare pyaase pyaasi raat suhaani
kaheen adhoori rah na jaaye apani prem kahaani 
saji sej suhaagan baji shahanaai aayi raat milan ki
kitne dinon ke baad ...tanhaai ke rusavaai ke ham kitne gam jhelen
kahane ko to saath rahen ham phir bhi rahen akele 
meri ho ke bhi rahi mujhase paraayi aayi raat milan ki
kitne dinon ke baad ... do roohon ke milan ka ye waqt guzar na jaaye
mehki mehki raat milan ki jaake phir na aaye
mil jaayenge ham raam duhaai aayi raat milan ki
kitne dinon ke baad ...
