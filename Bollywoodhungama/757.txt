O Mere Sapno Ke Saudagar
Dil Hai Ke Manta Nahin


O mere sapanon ke saudaagar, tuz ko muz se hain pyaar agar
to chal mere sang chal chal  mere sang aa aaur mere sang gaao mere sapanon ke saudaagar, muze ayesee jagah le jaa
main chaahatee hoo, mere humasafar muze pariyon kee duniyaan dikhaa
pyaar hee pyaar ho jis jagah, muze ayesaa jahaan dikhaaye pariyon kee bastee, sitaaron kaa majamaa
yahaa goonjataa hai, mohabbat kaa nagmaa
yahaa pyaar ke hain diwaane sabhee, chaahat hee sabakuchh yahaapariyon kee duniyaan ke mehumaan ho, chalo tum ko sair karaye
kahaa hain wo sapanon kaa saudaagar, chalo hum use dhoondh laayechandaa ke rath pe wo aayegaa yek din
muze saath leke wo jaayegaa yek din
meree maang bhar degaa taaron se wo, banaayegaa dulhan muze
