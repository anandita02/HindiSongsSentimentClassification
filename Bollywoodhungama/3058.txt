na tumako koi samajhe na hamako koi jaane
ham log aise niraale hain
dil jo bhi kah de vo karate hain
ham dekh ke jisako zinda hain
sach poochho to usape marate hain
ham hain jo manachale ham hain vo dilajale
jeevan ki raah mein jo door hain chale
o yaad karoge hamein
maan lo
kuchh ham bhi. maana hoge tum kaheen aur ham kaheen phir bhi gam nahi
jaayen chaahen ham kaheen aayengi vaheen yaaden sab haseen
yaaden jo aayengi kuch aise chhaayengi
beete din raat ki tasveeren laayengi
din raat in aankhon mein
maan lo

yaad aayega hamein apana ye gulasitaan

maan lo
