Aayee Milan Ki Raat


maine kisi ko dil de diya
dil de diya haan ye dil de diya 
poochho poochho poochho kaun haivo
bolo bolo bolo kaun hai vomaine kisi ka dil le liya 
dil le liya haan ye dil le liya 
poochho poochho poochho kaun hai vo 
bolo bolo bolo kaun hai vochhail chhabeela mera sapera usaka sajeela roop hai 
baaton mein usaki saragam ki khushbu chehare pe sooraj ki dhoop hai 
chain ganvaaya neend ganvaai jeena hai mushkil raam duhaai 
kisane ye mujhape jaadu kiya 
jaadu kiya haan jaadu kiya 
poochho poochho poochho kaun hai ...honthon pe usake shabanam ke moti
been ki jaisi patali kamar hai naagin ke jaisi chaal hai
rang gulaabi aankh nasheeli thodi si hai vo sharmeeli
jisane churaaya mera jiya 
mera jiya haan mera jiya 
poochho poochho poochho kaun hai ...
